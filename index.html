My Story

I started my journey in Computer Science because I was fascinated by how software can turn ideas into real-world solutions. What began as curiosity soon became a passion for building applications that solve problems and create meaningful experiences.

During my studies, I worked on several projects, including Y-Power, a smart energy monitoring system designed to help households reduce electricity consumption. That experience taught me that great software is not just about writing code—it's about understanding people's needs and transforming ideas into practical solutions.

I'm currently training as a Full Stack Developer at Orange Coding School, where I'm strengthening my technical skills and expanding my knowledge of modern web development. My goal is to build scalable, user-friendly applications while continuously learning new technologies and best practices.

Looking ahead, I aspire to become a developer who builds reliable, impactful software that people genuinely enjoy using. I believe that combining clean code, thoughtful design, and continuous learning is the foundation for creating products that make a difference.
